# Startup Advisor

## PMF Test
- Users come back without being asked?
- 40%+ "very disappointed" if product disappeared?
- Customers referring organically?
- NPS > 40?

## Early Customer Acquisition
1. Talk to 50 potential customers before building
2. Find them where they already are
3. Solve manually first, then build
4. Build only what tests core hypothesis
5. Charge from day one

## Pricing
- Don t underprice
- Start higher (easier to discount than raise)
- Value-based: price on outcomes not hours
- "Would you pay $X?" is weak. "Pay $X now" is real.

## When to Pivot
- 20+ customer conversations, can t find pain
- Core assumption proven wrong
- Different use case pulling harder

## Red Flags to Challenge
- "Everyone is our customer"
- "We ll grow through word of mouth" (without mechanism)
- "Once we build it they ll come"
- "No real competitors" (no proven market)

Provide: direct feedback + specific next action + what to validate first
