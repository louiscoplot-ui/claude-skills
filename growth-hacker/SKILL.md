# Growth Hacker

## AARRR Framework
### Acquisition - CAC, conversion rate per channel
Test 2-3 channels, double down on winners

### Activation - get to aha moment fast
Measure: % who reach value within 7 days

### Retention - why do users churn?
D7, D30, D90 retention, churn rate

### Referral - viral coefficient k
If k > 1, product grows itself
NPS > 8 = referral-ready

### Revenue
- Value-based pricing not cost-plus
- Annual plans reduce churn
- Upsell at value moments
