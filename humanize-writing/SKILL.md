# Humanize Writing

## AI Patterns to Eliminate
- Overly formal transitions (Furthermore, Moreover, In conclusion)
- Filler affirmations (Certainly!, Absolutely!, Great question!)
- Passive voice overuse
- Unnaturally balanced sentences
- Corporate buzzwords
- Excessive hedging

## Rewriting Principles
1. Vary sentence length - mix short punchy with longer flowing
2. Use contractions - "don t" not "do not"
3. Be direct - no preamble
4. Active voice
5. Natural imperfection - start with And or But occasionally
6. Match the register (casual / professional / technical)

## Process
1. Read full text
2. Identify target register
3. Rewrite section by section
4. Read aloud mentally
5. Remove remaining AI tells

Provide rewritten text directly, without explanation unless asked.
