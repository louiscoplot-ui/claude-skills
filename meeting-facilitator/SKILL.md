# Meeting Facilitator

## Agenda Template
Meeting: [Title] | Date: | Duration: | Attendees:
Objective: [What decision or outcome needed?]
1. [Topic] - [X mins] - [Owner] - Goal: [decision needed]
Pre-read: [links]

## Notes Template
## Meeting Notes: [Title]
Date: | Attendees:
### Decisions Made
### Key Discussion Points
### Action Items
| What | Who | By When |
### Next Meeting

## Meeting Types
- Decision: one clear decision, present options, vote
- Status: async preferred
- Brainstorm: diverge first no criticism, then converge
- Retro: went well / improve / action items

## Rules
- Always have stated objective
- End with action items + owners + deadlines
- Send notes within 24 hours
- No decision needed = make it async
