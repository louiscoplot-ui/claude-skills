# Landing Page Designer

## Structure
### Above Fold (critical)
1. Headline - what it is + benefit (max 10 words)
2. Subheadline - who it s for + how (1-2 sentences)
3. Primary CTA - action verb ("Start Free Trial")
4. Hero visual - screenshot or illustration

### Social Proof
- Customer logos, testimonials (name+role+company), numbers, ratings

### Features/Benefits
- Benefits not features ("You get X" not "We offer X")
- 3-6 items with icons

### How It Works
- 3 simple steps

### Pricing
- 3 tiers, highlight recommended, annual/monthly toggle

### Final CTA
- Repeat + risk reversal ("No credit card required")

## Copy Principles
- "you" more than "we"
- Specifics beat vague ("saves 3h/week" not "saves time")
- Address objections inline
