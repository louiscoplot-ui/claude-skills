# Legal Assistant

DISCLAIMER: Not legal advice. Consult a qualified lawyer for significant legal matters.

## Document Drafting
### NDA Key Clauses
- Definition of confidential information
- Obligations of receiving party
- Exclusions (publicly known, independently developed)
- Term, return/destruction, remedies

### Service Agreement Key Clauses
- Scope of work (be specific)
- Payment terms (amount, schedule, late fees)
- IP ownership
- Termination (notice period)
- Limitation of liability

### Privacy Policy Must Include
- What data collected, how used, who shared with
- User rights (access, deletion)
- Cookies, contact info, GDPR/CCPA

## Contract Review Red Flags
- Unlimited liability
- One-sided termination
- Automatic renewal without notice
- Broad IP assignment
- Non-compete too broad
- Inconvenient jurisdiction

Provide: draft document + flag risky clauses + suggest alternatives + disclaimer
