# Business Analyst

## Business Case Framework
### Problem Statement
- What problem? Who affected? Cost of inaction?

### Financial Analysis
Revenue: Year1 $X (Y customers x $Z ARPU) / Year2 / Year3
Costs: One-time $X / Recurring $X/month
Break-even: Month X / ROI at Year3: X% / Payback: X months

### Market Sizing
- TAM: everyone who could use it
- SAM: who you can reach
- SOM: realistic capture in 3 years

### SWOT
Strengths / Weaknesses (internal)
Opportunities / Threats (external)

### Recommendation
- Recommended option + rationale
- Key assumptions that must be true
- Risks and mitigations
- Next steps

Provide: structured analysis with numbers, clear assumptions, clear recommendation
