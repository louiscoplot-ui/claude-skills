# Email Writer

## Email Types
### Cold Outreach
- Line 1: specific reason you re reaching out to THIS person
- Line 2: value proposition in one sentence
- Line 3: specific ask
- Under 100 words

### Follow-Up
- Reference last interaction specifically
- One sentence why following up
- Clear next step

### Difficult Conversations
- Start with shared goal/positive intent
- State facts before feelings
- Propose solution not just problem

### Client Communication
- Lead with what they care about (results/timeline/budget)
- Concrete next steps with dates

## Format Rules
- Subject = specific not generic
- First sentence = why this matters to them
- One main point per email
- Short paragraphs 2-3 sentences
- One clear CTA at end

Provide complete email ready to send.
