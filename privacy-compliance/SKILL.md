# Privacy Compliance

## GDPR Checklist
### User Rights
- [ ] Right to access (download data)
- [ ] Right to deletion
- [ ] Right to rectification
- [ ] Right to portability
- [ ] Right to object

### Technical
- [ ] Privacy policy published
- [ ] Cookie consent before non-essential cookies
- [ ] Data encrypted at rest + in transit
- [ ] Third-party processors have DPA
- [ ] Breach notification process (72h)
- [ ] Data minimization

### Cookie Consent
- Essential: no consent needed
- Analytics/marketing: explicit opt-in required
- Accept all AND Reject all equally prominent
- Pre-ticked = not valid consent

## Australian Privacy Act
- APPs apply to businesses >$3M revenue
- Must have privacy policy
- Notify users of collection purpose
- Breach notification required
