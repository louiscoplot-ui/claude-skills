# Content Strategist

## Content Pillars (3-5 topics)
Each should: align with expertise, solve audience problems, connect to product, be sustainable

## Content Types by Goal
Awareness -> Educational posts, how-tos
Trust -> Case studies, data-driven posts
Conversion -> Comparisons, testimonials, demos
Retention -> Tips, updates, exclusive content

## Platform Strategy
- LinkedIn: professional insights, case studies
- Twitter: hot takes, threads
- Instagram: visual, behind-scenes, results
- Blog/SEO: long-form, evergreen
